Daal Fry

Description

Daal Fry is a popular Indian lentil dish made with toor dal and flavorful spices. It’s creamy, comforting, and perfect with rice or roti.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup toor dal
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 inch ginger
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander

Steps

  1. Boil the toor dal with turmeric until soft.
  2. In a pan, heat ghee and add cumin seeds.
  3. Add chopped onions, ginger, garlic and sauté.
  4. Mix in tomatoes and cook until mushy.
  5. Add the cooked dal and simmer for 10 minutes.
  6. Garnish with coriander and serve hot.

Description

Maggi is a quick and delicious instant noodle snack that has become a staple in Indian households. Known for its unique masala flavor and easy preparation, Maggi is perfect for breakfast, lunch, or a midnight craving. It’s ready in just 2 minutes and can be customized with veggies, cheese, or eggs for a more filling meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 packet Maggi noodles
  • 1 ½ cups water
  • 1 sachet Maggi tastemaker (comes with the packet)
  • 1 tsp oil or butter (optional)
  • Chopped vegetables (like onion, tomato, capsicum) – optional

Steps

  1. Pour 1½ cups of water into a pan and bring it to a boil on medium heat.
  2. Break the Maggi noodle cake into 2-3 pieces and add it to the boiling water.
  3. Open the Maggi tastemaker sachet and sprinkle the entire contents into the pan
  4. Stir gently so that the tastemaker mixes evenly with the water and noodles
  5. If using, add chopped vegetables and cheese
  6. Let it simmer for 2–3 minutes. Stir occasionally until most of the water is absorbed and noodles are cooked.
  7. Turn off the heat and serve your hot and tasty Maggi!

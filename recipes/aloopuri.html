Aloo Puri

Description

Aloo Puri is a classic North Indian dish served for breakfast or lunch. It consists of spicy potato curry with deep-fried puffed puris.

Ingredients

  • 2 boiled potatoes
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 1 tomato
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • Wheat flour (for puri)
  • Salt & oil

Steps

  1. Heat oil, add cumin, onion and saut√©.
  2. Add tomatoes and cook until soft.
  3. Add mashed potatoes and spices.
  4. Cook for 5-10 minutes with some water.
  5. For puris, knead dough and roll into small discs.
  6. Fry puris until golden and puffed. Serve with curry.

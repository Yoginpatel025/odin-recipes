Pasta

Description

A quick and cheesy pasta recipe that’s perfect for dinner. Made with penne pasta, fresh veggies, and creamy sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup penne pasta
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1/2 cup bell peppers
  • 1/2 cup tomato puree
  • 1/4 cup cream or cheese
  • Salt and pepper
  • Oregano & chili flakes

Steps

  1. Boil pasta until soft. Drain and keep aside.
  2. Sauté onion and bell peppers in olive oil.
  3. Add tomato puree and spices. Cook well.
  4. Mix in cream/cheese and stir until creamy.
  5. Add pasta and toss everything together.
  6. Serve hot with chili flakes and oregano.
